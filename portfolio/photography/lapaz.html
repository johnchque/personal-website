Far away from the colors I've always known, I feel the air clear and cold. Everytime I'm at home, life seems to stop for a bit once again. This time, though, I have the chance to stop along.
Escaping from the endless buildings, we make a stop around the mountains. Near Wara Warani, I see around and look up at myself. All things I've seen all my life, are presented in immediate sight to the naked eye. The brown of the nature and the blue of the sky present the combination I always loved from this landscape.
I hope for myself to dream again, in the circles that my mind escapes for now. As the distance grows and these place forms an image in my mind, I'm ready for what's to come. I exist in the mountains of La Paz.

I read a poem for what we long. And wonder if all the weight will not be an obstacle anymore. I walk for longer to see the answer and reach for the places I never thought of.
One memory will cross my mind through the clouds that follow me. I feel peace at last.
