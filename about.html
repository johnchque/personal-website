Hi, I am John Choque

I make YouTube videos, host a podcast, and write a weekly newsletter called Weekly Realizations.

I make videos about mental health, productivity, scholarships, and everything in between. My weekly newsletter contains information about my weekly findings and reflections about life. All to live a full life.

If you want to see my articles and newsletter issues, you'll find all of them here.

Besides that, I host a podcast with my friend Halima, called The Thoughtful Millennial. This podcast follows two self-proclaimed thoughtful millennials in their relentless journeys to becoming more socially conscious humans. And they draw from these experiences (amongst others) to present refreshing views on the big philosophical questions of our times. The perspectives shared are drawn from diverse cultural, economic and professional backgrounds which molded the choices that led them to today.

Beyond YouTube and my podcast, I also work on other projects about sustainability.

Currently, I contribute to OPENschool Solutions. Creating a platform that will help introduce the OPENschool method in the current education system. The primer is to allow students to guide their own learning and have teachers as supporters of this process.

Also, one of the most challenging initiatives I started is called #historiasquenovemos, where we aim to normalize the male emotional expression so we learn to look for help. We want more men to take care of their mental health. And this is mainly focused on Latin American societies.
