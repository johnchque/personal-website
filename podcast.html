The Thoughtful Millennial

The podcast where we reflect on our rapidly evolving attitudes on everything from mental health to how to become a more sustainable human.

The Thoughtful Millennial is a podcast about two self-proclaimed thoughtful millennials in their relentless journeys to becoming more socially conscious humans.

The two unlikely friends chose very different paths: social entrepreneurship versus white-collar work; and they draw from these experiences (amongst others) to present refreshing views on the big philosophical questions of our times. The perspectives shared are drawn from diverse cultural, economic and professional backgrounds which molded the choices that led them to today.
